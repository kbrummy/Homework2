About Me

My name is Kristin Brumfield. I live in the Sacramento area and I am taking the UC Davis Coding Bootcamp. My goal for this course is to become a full stack web developer and begin a career in that field.

Currently I work full time at Sacramento Natural Foods Co-Op. Before that, my work experience has been primarily in the fitness/gym realm, rock climbing specifically. I started rock climbing in 2008 and have since worked at two different rock climbing gyms in the Sacramento area. Although rock climbing is still a huge passion of mine, I decided I need to move on from it as a career.

When I am not working or coding, you can find me doing any one of my other passions: rock climbing, hiking, paddle boarding, yoga, reading, creating, or spending time with friends.
